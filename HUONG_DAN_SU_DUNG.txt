VIETSUB STUDIO - HUONG DAN CAI DAT VA SU DUNG
================================================

1. CHAY APP
- Giai nen file ZIP vao mot thu muc rieng.
- Mo "VietSub Studio.exe".
- Ban EXE se tu tao shortcut "VietSub Studio" ngoai Desktop o lan mo dau tien.
- Neu shortcut bi xoa hoac ban chuyen thu muc app, bam "Tao lai shortcut ngoai Desktop" trong phan Thiet lap.
- Lan mo dau co the mat 20-60 giay do Windows giai nen va quet bao mat file EXE.
- Neu Windows SmartScreen hien canh bao vi app chua co chu ky so, chon More info > Run anyway.

2. CAC THANH PHAN CAN CO TREN MAY
- Windows 10/11 va Microsoft Edge.
- Neu dung link Douyin: cai Douzy o thu muc mac dinh, mo it nhat mot lan va chon thu muc tai video.
- VideOCR duoc cai tai: C:\Program Files\VideOCR\videocr-cli.exe
- Tuy chon: FFmpeg/FFprobe trong PATH de quy doi vung crop OCR chinh xac hon.

3. THIET LAP LAN DAU
- Trong VietSub Studio, mo tab "Thiet lap".
- Dan link Gemini Notebook cua ban, dang:
  https://gemini.google.com/notebook/ID-CUA-BAN
- Kiem tra cac muc bat buoc. Khi tat ca muc mau do da het, app san sang.
- De bat "Chay Edge an" neu muon. Edge van render binh thuong nhung duoc thu nho.
- App chi tu hien Edge khi thay man hinh dang nhap hoac khong tim thay khung chat sau khi cho Notebook tai xong.
- Khi app tu hien Edge, kiem tra tai khoan Google va Notebook roi bam chay lai job.

4. XU LY VIDEO
- Dan link/video share Douyin hoac bam "Chon MP4" de nhan video tren may.
- Nhap ten bo file neu muon. Neu de trong, app dung ten video Douyin.
- Bam "Tai preview", sau do keo va doi kich thuoc khung vang vao vung phu de.
- Bam "Them vao hang doi" va lap lai voi cac video khac.
- Bam "Chay hang doi". App se tu xu ly tung video mot, khong chay song song.
- Hang doi duoc luu khi dong app. Job loi/da huy co the chay lai tu video, OCR hoac dich neu file trung gian con ton tai.
- Ket qua duoc dat ten dong bo:
  Video Douyin: dung truc tiep file MP4 Douzy da tai, khong tao them ban sao.
  Video local: Ten video.mp4
  Ten video.thumbnail.jpg/png/webp (neu nguon co thumbnail)
  Ten video.raw.srt
  Ten video.vi.srt
- App chi bao "da san sang" khi ca ba file ton tai va sub Viet vuot qua kiem tra timestamp/so doan.
- Job loi van co nut mo thu muc lam do, nhung khong duoc gan nhan la ket qua hoan tat.

5. LUU Y KHI GUI CHO NGUOI KHAC
- File EXE chua VietSub Studio, nhung khong kem Douzy, VideOCR hay tai khoan Gemini.
- Moi nguoi dung phai tu cai cac thanh phan tren va dung Notebook Gemini cua ho.
- Cau hinh cua moi may duoc luu rieng trong %APPDATA%\VietSub Studio.
- Khi co phien ban moi, bam "Tai & cai ngay". App se tai ZIP chinh thuc, kiem tra SHA256, thay EXE va tu mo lai.
- Neu khong the tu cai (vi du thu muc khong co quyen ghi), app se mo GitHub Releases de cap nhat thu cong.
