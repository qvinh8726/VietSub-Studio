VIETSUB STUDIO - HUONG DAN CAI DAT VA SU DUNG
================================================

1. CHAY APP
- Giai nen file ZIP vao mot thu muc rieng.
- Mo "VietSub Studio.exe".
- Lan mo dau co the mat 20-60 giay do Windows giai nen va quet bao mat file EXE.
- Neu Windows SmartScreen hien canh bao vi app chua co chu ky so, chon More info > Run anyway.

2. CAC THANH PHAN CAN CO TREN MAY
- Windows 10/11 va Microsoft Edge.
- Douzy duoc cai o thu muc mac dinh. Mo Douzy it nhat mot lan va chon thu muc tai video.
- VideOCR duoc cai tai: C:\Program Files\VideOCR\videocr-cli.exe
- Tuy chon: FFmpeg/FFprobe trong PATH de quy doi vung crop OCR chinh xac hon.

3. THIET LAP LAN DAU
- Trong VietSub Studio, mo tab "Thiet lap".
- Dan link Gemini Notebook cua ban, dang:
  https://gemini.google.com/notebook/ID-CUA-BAN
- Kiem tra cac muc bat buoc. Khi tat ca muc mau do da het, app san sang.
- Khi app mo Edge rieng, dang nhap tai khoan Google co quyen truy cap Notebook.

4. XU LY VIDEO
- Dan link video Douyin hoac ca doan noi dung share.
- Nhap ten bo file neu muon. Neu de trong, app dung ten video Douyin.
- Ket qua duoc dat ten dong bo:
  Ten video.mp4
  Ten video.raw.srt
  Ten video.vi.srt

5. LUU Y KHI GUI CHO NGUOI KHAC
- File EXE chua VietSub Studio, nhung khong kem Douzy, VideOCR hay tai khoan Gemini.
- Moi nguoi dung phai tu cai cac thanh phan tren va dung Notebook Gemini cua ho.
- Cau hinh cua moi may duoc luu rieng trong %APPDATA%\VietSub Studio.
